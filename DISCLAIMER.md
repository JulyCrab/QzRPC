# Legal Disclaimer & Privacy

## Affiliation

This application is an unofficial third-party tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Qobuz, Discord, Last.fm, Apple Inc., or iTunes.

## How We Use Your Data

### Discord Client ID
Used only to connect to Discord's Rich Presence API. Stored locally on your device.

### Last.fm API Key
Used to fetch album artwork. Stored locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers.

### Qobuz Activity
Monitored locally on your device to display what you're listening to. No listening data is collected or transmitted by us.

### Album Artwork
Album artwork is fetched from Last.fm and iTunes Search API. These services may have their own data collection policies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
- [Last.fm Privacy Policy](https://www.last.fm/legal/privacy)
- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### No Analytics
We don't collect usage statistics, telemetry, or any personal information.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in: `%AppData%\QzRPC\settings.json`

## Third-Party Services

This application makes use of the following third-party services:

- **iTunes Search API** - For fetching album artwork as a fallback
